The Freya Vivarium Control System

Freya is an advanced, automated control system designed specifically for vivariums, terrariums, and paludariums. Built on the Raspberry Pi platform, it provides precise environmental monitoring and control to create the perfect habitat for your plants and animals.

Key Features:

  • Real-time temperature and humidity monitoring
  • Automated lighting control with customizable schedules
  • Misting and irrigation system management
  • Web-based dashboard for remote monitoring
  • Data logging and environmental analytics
  • Modular and expandable design

Whether you're maintaining a tropical rainforest environment or a desert habitat, Freya provides the automation and precision you need to keep your vivarium thriving.

Your Vivarium. Your Control. Your Freedom.

With Freya, you have an assistant that controls the environment for you – while you stay fully in control of how your vivarium works, evolves, and grows.

Open by Design

Freya is fully open source – hardware, software, and enclosure designs. You can read the code, study the schematics, and adapt the 3D-printable case to your own builds. That openness isn't a marketing line; it's the guarantee that you're never stuck with a black box that only one company can fix or extend.

Built to Be Repaired, Not Replaced

From standard components to the printable enclosure, Freya is designed to survive time, not a product cycle. If something breaks, you (or your local builder) can open it, diagnose it, and swap parts instead of replacing the whole unit. Documentation lives in the open, so every repair adds to shared knowledge instead of disappearing into a service ticket.

Real Ownership, on Your Terms

Freya runs on your hardware, in your home. There are no mandatory accounts, no subscription wall, no remote kill switch. You can keep everything fully local, or connect to a trusted “cloud keeper” who runs the web interface for you – without giving up control of your data or your tank. The system works for you, not the other way around.

Responsible Stewardship Made Easier

Freya is built around one simple idea: happy animals need a stable, well-understood environment. By watching the climate, logging what happens and handling routine adjustments, Freya lowers the barrier to responsible wildlife keeping in tanks – making good stewardship easier to achieve and repeat.
